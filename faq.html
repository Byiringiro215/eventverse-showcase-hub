Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventVerse

EventVerse is a comprehensive event discovery and registration platform that connects event organizers with attendees worldwide. We make it easy to find, register for, and attend events that match your interests, from tech conferences to art exhibitions and everything in between.

You can find events using our search and filter tools on the Events page. Search by keywords, filter by category, location, date, and price range. You can also browse featured events on our homepage or get personalized recommendations based on your interests.

Yes, browsing and discovering events on EventVerse is completely free. You only pay when you register for paid events. Free events remain free, and we don't charge any additional fees beyond what the event organizer sets.

Yes, you need to create a free EventVerse account to register for events. This allows you to manage your registrations, receive event updates, and get personalized recommendations. Creating an account takes less than a minute!

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Yes, you can delete your account at any time from your account settings. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to cancel any upcoming event registrations before deleting your account.

Simply find the event you want to attend, click "Register Now," fill out the registration form, and complete payment if required. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket and event details immediately after successful registration.

Ticket transfer policies depend on the event organizer. Some events allow transfers while others don't. Check the event details page or contact the organizer directly for their specific transfer policy.

If an event is cancelled, you'll be notified immediately via email and receive a full refund within 5-7 business days. The event organizer may also offer alternative dates or virtual participation options.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Refund policies are set by individual event organizers. Most events offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event, partial refunds between 30-14 days, and no refunds within 14 days. Check the specific event's refund policy before registering.

EventVerse does not charge additional service fees to attendees. The price you see is the price you pay. However, standard payment processing fees may apply depending on your payment method and location.

EventVerse works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience. Our platform is also mobile-friendly and works great on smartphones and tablets.

First, try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try accessing your account from a different browser or device. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team at support@eventverse.com with details about the issue you're experiencing.

Currently, EventVerse is available as a responsive web application that works perfectly on mobile devices. We're working on dedicated iOS and Android apps, which will be available soon. Follow us on social media for updates!
